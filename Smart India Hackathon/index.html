OVERVIEW

Education is the cornerstone of a thriving society, but the challenge of school dropout rates persists. Our website’s goal is to analyse the real time raw data, make it understandable for the public with the help of patterns and insights but with way better visualization so that it can be understood even by an amateur.

OUR VISION

Our website have a lot of key features, some of them are:
• It explores data through interactive charts and graphs
• It categorizes data according to the person’s need
• Helpful for educators, researchers and many more.
• We used real data so no issues of authentication can be raised.
